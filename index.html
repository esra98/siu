WELCOME TO SIU 2023

EUSIPCO is the premier European conference on signal processing and offers a comprehensive technical program addressing all the latest developments in research and technology for signal processing. The 31st edition of EUSIPCO will be held in Helsinki, the vivid capitol city of Finland. Helsinki is well-known of its sea-facing landscape, diverse architecture, world-famous design, and Nordic cuisine. The conference will be held at Scandic Grand Marina Congress Center, located in beautiful harbor area of the city center of Helsinki that is only a short walking distance from the historic district (the senate square) or presidential palace. The EUSIPCO 2023 technical program will include keynotes by distinguished speakers, tutorials and special sessions, and cutting-edge research presentations on topics that are driving the information revolution and signal processing today.
